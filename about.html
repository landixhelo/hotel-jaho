About Us

HOTEL JAHO

THE BEST HOTEL IN SPILLE

Situated just 1.3 km from Spille Beach, Hotel Jaho provides accommodation in Kavajë with access to a garden, a terrace, as well as full-day security. Featuring a shared kitchen, this property also provides guests with a picnic area. The apartment features garden views, a children's playground, a 24-hour front desk, and free WiFi is available throughout the property.

The apartment complex will provide guests with air-conditioned units with a wardrobe, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a balcony and a private bathroom with a bidet. Units include a patio boasting outdoor furniture. The units are equipped with heating facilities.

Rock of Kavaje is 32 km from Hotel Jaho, while Durres Amphiteatre is 43 km from the property. The nearest airport is Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 68 km from the accommodation.
